The Editorial Board of the journal “Problems of Artificial Intelligence” reviews all the received manuscripts corresponding to the journal subject area in order to make their expert appraisal and to ensure their scientific validity and editorial standards. Scientific papers (articles, reviews, etc.) obtain an individual registration number and any following progress is recorded by responsible members of the editorial staff.

The journal practices the peer review by competent specialists, members of the Editorial Board and scientists from detached organizations by arrangement. All the reviewers are recognized experts in corresponding scientific areas of proposed manuscripts and have publications in the same field as a reviewing paper for the last 3 years. The reviews are kept in the editorial office for 5 years. The reviewers follow the standards and principles of professional ethics based on the Code approved by the Committee on Publication Ethics.

The Editorial Board of the journal is in active collaboration with scientists from major scientific Institutes of Russia, Byelorussia, Kazakhstan, Poland, and other countries, in this connection the processes of reviewing and publication agreement often take place on-line or by e-mail.
