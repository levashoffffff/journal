b>Annotation: The article considers the classification of mental states and classifications of human emotional states. The interrelationships of mental states and the stress effect on each of them are analyzed. Selected the most important mental states for diagnosis in the event of a random nature deviations in the operation of an automated control system to accelerate the decision-making system. Keywords: automated control system, emergency, mental state, emotional state, classification.
