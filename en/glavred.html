Shelepov Vladislav Yurievich (14.04. 1943) – Dr. Phys.-Math., professor.

Biography

Shelepov V. Y. was born 14 April 1943, studied and graduated from the Donetsk Polytechnic Institute in 1973. In 1990 he defended the doctoral dissertation “On Integral Equations along the Radon Contours and Boundary Properties of Elliptical Equations and Systems in Radon Spatial Domains”.

Scientific Activity

Donetsk State University, North Ossetian State University, Donetsk Commercial University, Donetsk National Technical University, Donetsk State Institute of Artificial Intelligence. A member of the Editorial Board of the journal “Artificial Intelligence”. Awarded by the Certificate of Honour of the National Academy of Science of Ukraine.

At the present time Shelepov V.Y. is the chief research officer of the Public Institution “Institute of Problems of Artificial Intelligence”, the chief editor of the journal “Problems of Artificial Intelligence” (Donetsk), a member of the editorial board of the journal “Speech technology” (“Rechevie Tehnologii”) (Moscow, Russia) and the journal “Eurasian Journal of Mathematical and Computer Applications” (Kazakhstan).

State Awards

«Honoured Science and Technology Worker», «Honoured Education Worker».

Field of Scientific Interests

Artificial Intelligence, speech technologies.
